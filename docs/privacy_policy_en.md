# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 10, 2026**

Portfiq ("Service") values your privacy and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act and the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection of the Republic of Korea.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Collected Items

| Category | Item | Collection Method |
|----------|------|-------------------|
| Automatic | Device ID (auto-generated unique identifier) | Generated upon app installation |
| User Input | Registered ETF list | Entered by user |
| Automatic | App usage event logs | Collected during app usage |
| Automatic | Push notification token | Collected when notifications are enabled |

### Information We Do Not Collect

This Service does not collect directly identifiable personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or national identification number. There is no sign-up process. The Device ID is an automatically generated unique identifier created upon app installation.

---

## 2. Purpose of Collection and Use

| Item | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Device ID | User identification and personalized briefing delivery |
| Registered ETF list | Generating customized news briefings based on ETFs of interest |
| App usage event logs | Service quality improvement and error analysis |
| Push notification token | Sending morning briefings, night checkpoints, and urgent news alerts |

---

## 3. Retention Period

- **Principle**: Information is retained until the purpose of collection is fulfilled.
- **Upon app deletion (account termination)**: Destroyed within **30 days** of the deletion request.
- **Event logs**: Retained for **90 days** from the date of collection, then automatically destroyed.

---

## 4. Sharing with Third Parties

This Service entrusts personal information processing to the following third parties.

| Service Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|-----------------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase Inc. | Database hosting and storage | Device ID, registered ETF list, event logs |
| Anthropic (Claude API) | AI-powered news analysis and briefing generation | Registered ETF list (upon analysis request) |
| Google Firebase | Push notification delivery | Push notification token |

Each service provider is prohibited from using personal information beyond the entrusted purpose and manages data securely in accordance with applicable laws.

---

## 5. Destruction of Personal Information

- **Procedure**: Personal information is destroyed without delay upon expiration of the retention period or achievement of the processing purpose.
- **Methods**:
  - Electronic files: Permanently deleted using methods that prevent recovery
  - Physical records: Shredded or incinerated

---

## 6. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

- **Request for deletion**: Via [Settings > Delete Data] in the app or by email at support@portfiq.com
- **Request for access**: By email at support@portfiq.com
- **Request for processing suspension**: By email at support@portfiq.com

Results will be communicated within **10 days** of receiving the request.

---

## 7. Security Measures

The following measures are implemented to ensure the security of personal information:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Minimized database access privileges
- Access log retention and monitoring

---

## 8. AI Service Disclosure

This Service uses AI (Claude by Anthropic) to analyze news and generate briefings.

- AI-generated content is labeled with an "AI Analysis" indicator.
- AI analysis results are **reference information, not investment advice**.
- The registered ETF list may be transmitted to the AI for generating personalized briefings.
