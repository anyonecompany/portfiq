포트픽은 서학 ETF 투자자를 위한 AI 브리핑 앱입니다.

✅ 내가 가진 ETF 기준으로 맞춤 뉴스를 분석해줍니다
✅ 매일 아침 8:35, 간밤 미장 결과를 3줄로 정리
✅ 오늘 밤 체크포인트도 미리 알려줍니다

## App Description (English)

Portfiq is an AI-powered briefing app for US-listed ETF investors.

✅ Personalized news analysis based on your ETF portfolio
✅ Daily morning briefing at 8:35 AM — overnight US market summary in 3 lines
✅ Tonight's market checkpoints delivered before market opens
✅ AI evaluates whether news is bullish or bearish for your ETFs
✅ Sector concentration warnings and macro sensitivity analysis

Supporting 30+ popular ETFs including QQQ, VOO, SCHD, TQQQ, SOXL.
AI analyzes. You decide.

⚠️ Portfiq's AI analysis is for informational purposes only, not investment advice.
